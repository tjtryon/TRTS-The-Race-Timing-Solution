Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is this website for?
A: This site lets you view individual and team race results for past events. You can also contact the organizers or get help using this page.

Q: How do I find my individual race results?
A: Go to the home page and click on the event date and race number. Then click on the Individual Results link to see your bib number, name, and finish time.

Q: I don’t see my name or bib number. What should I do?
A: This could mean your bib was not scanned or assigned correctly. Please contact the race organizer to have it corrected.

Q: My finish time looks incorrect. Can it be changed?
A: If you believe there is a mistake, please contact race staff with your bib number and any supporting details.

Q: How are team scores calculated?
A: Teams are scored based on the finish positions of their top 5 runners. Lower scores are better.

Q: Why isn't my team listed in the team results?
A: Teams only appear if there are at least 2 or more runners from the same team. Make sure your bib was registered with the correct team name.

Q: What’s the difference between Individual Results and Team Results?
A: Individual Results show times for each runner. Team Results group runners by team and calculate team rankings based on their performance.

Q: How do I get back to the main page?
A: You can click “Home” in the navigation bar or return to the site’s main index from any page.

Q: Can I download or print my results?
A: Yes! You can use your browser’s print option (usually Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to save or print the results page.

Q: I have another question that’s not listed here. What should I do?
A: Please visit the Contact Us page to send a message to the race organizers.
